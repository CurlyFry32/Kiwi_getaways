Kiwi Getaways

Kiwi Getaways Established in 2009 in the picturesque region of Waikato, New Zealand, Kiwi Getaways has been dedicated to curating and showcasing the most unique and unforgettable holiday accommodations across the country. Our mission is simple: to provide travelers with access to New Zealand's hidden gems and extraordinary experiences.

Our Vision

Our Vision We believe that every journey should be marked by discovery and delight. From luxury treehouses nestled amidst lush forests to charming seaside villas overlooking pristine beaches, our handpicked selection of accommodations promises something extraordinary for every traveler.

What we offer

Finding and booking exceptional accommodations has never been easier. Our intuitive platform allows travelers to explore a diverse range of properties, each vetted for quality, comfort, and uniqueness. Whether you're seeking adventure or relaxation, we're here to make your dream getaway a reality.
